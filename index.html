Srinivasa Ramanujan was a brilliant Indian mathematician who made extraordinary contributions to number theory, mathematical analysis, infinite series, and continued fractions, despite having almost no formal training. Known for his intuition and ability to produce profound results, he is famous for his work on concepts like the Hardy-Ramanujan number (\(1729\))
